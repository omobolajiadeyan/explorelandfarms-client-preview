Who We Are

Sustainable practices, quality products, and serene living.

A working land company in Ibadan, Oyo State — forestry, cattle, markets, and real estate, grown from the same philosophy.

Our Story

From one plot in Ibadan to four working verticals.

ExploreLand began with a simple belief: land creates more value when forestry, food production, livestock, markets, and homes are planned as one connected system.

Today, our work spans four practical verticals—responsible forestry, naturally raised cattle, traceable farm-to-table markets, and thoughtful property development. Each one is shaped by the same commitment to healthy land, useful products, and lasting community value.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in creating sustainable and harmonious landscapes where agriculture, forestry, and real estate coalesce seamlessly.

Our Mission

Promoting ecological stewardship and responsible land use through sustainable agro-forestry and innovative real estate development — empowering landowners, building educational communities, and addressing deforestation through sustainable practice.
